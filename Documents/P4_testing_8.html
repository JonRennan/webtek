Testing procedure

To test the website we gave the test subject the url and let them choose whether to use their phone or computer. We then let them use the site freely while observing them. After they had used the site we gave them the google form linked below, to get their initial thoughts in writing. The form then gave them some tasks to complete and give specific feedback upon. They then gave their final opinion about the site.

The google form we used: here

Tester audience

We tested the webpage on the intendent audience, but not in the condition we think they might be when they would use the webpage. We used live testing with a few students. We gave them the url to the webpage, observed they use the webpage and sent them the google form to fill out afterwards.
